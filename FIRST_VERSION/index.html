Welcome

Welcome to Legacy High School Poms!

We are a united Varsity and JV family built on discipline, artistry, and excellence. Gold is our spark, navy is our foundation, and every routine is a celebration of school spirit.

Powerful performances & uplifting culture

From Friday night lights to regional showcases, the Poms deliver sharp technique, precision visuals, and a culture of support. Legacy athletes train with intention, lead with character, and uplift every event they attend.

Explore our gallery, follow our social channels, and celebrate the lightning bolt tradition that keeps our community cheering.

Mission

Our mission is to champion spirit and excellence

Legacy Poms Mission Statement

The mission of the spirit organization at Legacy High School shall be to promote school spirit and our loyalty for the athletic, fine arts, and academic programs within the community. Participation in a spirit organization provides opportunities for performance experience at local, state, and regional levels; successful personal growth; citizenship; leadership and life-long learning.

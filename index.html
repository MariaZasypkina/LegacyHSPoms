About the team

Legacy HS Poms Mission

The mission of the spirit organization at Legacy High School shall be to promote school spirit and our loyalty for the athletic, fine arts, and academic programs within the community. Participation in a spirit organization provides opportunities for performance experience at local, state, and national levels; successful personal growth; citizenship; leadership and life-long learning.
